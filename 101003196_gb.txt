George Brown College is a public and fully accredited college of applied arts & technology with total 3 campuses in Toronto, and many more in Ontario. 

The college was established in 1967 and had 6 presidents over the course of its operation. The current president is Gervan Fearon

Most notable campuses in Toronto are:

- Casa Loma Campus located in and around Kendal ave between davenport rd and MacPherson Ave.

- St. James Campus located near 200 King St. E., 290, and 300 Adelaide St. East.

- Waterfront Campus is located at 51 Dockside Dr.
